# PHASE 0: PROPOSAL SUMMARY - FindER

## What is it?
FindER is an Earthquake Emergency Response Platform designed to connect earthquake survivors, volunteers, emergency responders, and businesses. Its primary goal is to facilitate the real-time coordination of resources, safety statuses, and emergency aid during a disaster.

## Who are the users?
1. **Survivors**: Individuals in need of immediate assistance (food, shelter, medical aid).
2. **Volunteers**: People offering their help, including search-and-rescue efforts.
3. **Emergency Responders**: Official and professional rescue teams.
4. **Businesses**: Entities that can donate resources or offer relevant services.
5. **Moderators**: Administrators managing the platform to ensure smooth operation and safety.

## What problems does it solve?
1. **Information fragmentation**: Consolidates critical data into a single platform.
2. **Resource mismatch**: Ensures that available resources efficiently reach those who need them most.
3. **Family communication breakdown**: Provides a centralized status board to help families locate each other.
4. **Coordination chaos**: Organizes chaotic emergency situations with structured tools for responders and volunteers.
5. **Unverified misinformation**: Utilizes moderation and authoritative sources to provide verified information.

## Every Feature
1. **Multi-role auth**: Secure authentication using JWT and bcrypt, supporting different user roles.
2. **Emergency reporting**: Ability to report emergencies with GPS coordinates, photos, and severity levels.
3. **Resource finder**: A map-based tool with categories, reservation capabilities, and QR codes for tracking and claiming resources.
4. **Safety status board**: A public board where users can mark "I am safe" or report someone as missing/found.
5. **Volunteer coordination**: Matching volunteers based on skills, assigning tasks, and organizing teams.
6. **Responder dashboard**: A specialized view with heatmaps and resource allocation tools for official responders.
7. **Business portal**: A dedicated interface for businesses to verify themselves and offer donations or services.
8. **Real-time chat + push + SOS alerts**: Instant communication channels, push notifications, and emergency SOS triggers.
9. **Offline maps + navigation + hazard zones**: Navigation capabilities and hazard warnings that work without an active internet connection.
10. **Analytics**: System-wide data analysis including heatmaps and response time tracking.
11. **Content moderation**: Tools for manual flagging and AI-driven automated moderation to keep the platform safe.
12. **Offline capabilities**: Robust offline functionality using cached maps, Hive storage, and SMS fallbacks.
